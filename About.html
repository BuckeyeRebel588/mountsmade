About Me

May 29, 2022 by Brittany


Hello there! My name is Brittany Mounts, and I am the maker behind Mounts Made. It all started in 2016 when my dear friend Brandi bought me a crochet kit. Brandi is a fantastic crocheter! I wanted to try this new craft that I'd seen Brandi, my aunts, and my cousins do when I was a girl. See below for my first ever crochet swatch.


I was so proud! Now I can see the uneveness of my stitches, and my tension is all over the place - but it doesn't matter! I did it! I am now a crocheter. I eventually started trying more difficult items, and with the help of YouTube I was off and running.
